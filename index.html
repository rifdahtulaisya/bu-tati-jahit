The Tailor

Mrs. Tati Jahit, whose real name is Rohati, is a housewife. He first sewed at the age of 13. Mrs Tati initially tried sewing with a machine that was in her house at that time.

Mrs Tati first worked at the age of 16 in a clothing factory confectionery, located in Dramaga. He worked there for 5 years and was equipped with knowledge about sewing after he stopped working. Mrs Tati started her business when she just got married. Previously, the sewing place was in the kitchen area, and now it has moved to the 2nd floor of the house.

How to Order

Communication

Carrying out closer communication, it is recommended to meet in person because the process of making clothes is not as short as when you want to buy clothes. direct communication does not involve the risk of making mistakes in making the clothes.

Model

Clothing Model Consultation. Usually those who order have already prepared what model they want. Not only the model, but body size needs to be measured directly.

Clothing Material

Determining materials for clothes is not that easy. Negotiations are needed so that the materials chosen are not wrong. and the size will fit the model.
